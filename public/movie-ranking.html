The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spirited Away

This is my favourite anime. I love the beautiful images.

Pretty Little Liars

Such an intriguing show. The suspense keeps you on the edge of your seat, and the twists are so cleverly done!

Princess Protection Program

Such a charming movie. The friendship between the two leads is really heartwarming, and the storyline is so fun and uplifting!
